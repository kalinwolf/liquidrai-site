How the token projects are structured

Each project uses a simple structure: a real operating business produces revenue; distributions are made based on actual net profit. Tokens are designed as profit-participation instruments (not equity, not governance, not a promise).

Operational layer

  • Real production (food, commodities, infrastructure, etc.)
  • Measurable milestones and reporting cadence
  • Cash management and reserves

Token layer

  • Centralized issuance and administration (single authority)
  • Distributions based on actual profit, per published policy
  • Early-phase distributions may be issued in additional units (token) to preserve operating cash
